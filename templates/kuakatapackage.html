Dhaka - Kuakata - Dhaka Tour Package (by Bus)

Tour Duration : 4 Nights 3 Days (2 Nights stay at Kuakata)

Room / Hotel : Standard / Deluxe Accommodation on Twin Sharing basis as per the itinerary.

Day 01:

Leave Dhaka for Kuakata at 08:00 pm by Bus. This will be an overnight journey.

Day 02:

Hotel check-in and refresh. Go to beach to enjoy sea. After lunch go to Rakhain Tribe village to see their lifestyle. Back to beach and enjoy sunset.

Day 03:

Enjoy fabulous beauty of sunrise from the beach in early morning. Go to Fatra Mangrove forest. Watch the beauty of sunset from beach. This is the only sea beach in Bangladesh where you can enjoy sunrise and sunset from the same beach.

Day 04:

Enjoy sunrise from the beach. After breakfast visit Gangamoti Resurve Forest. After lunch take a short rest and then go to Jelepara (fishermen's village) to watch their lifestyle. After Dinner The bus will start for Dhaka. Next morning, We will be in Dhaka.

- End of Tour -

Note: We need minimum 4 pax to execute this tour program.
