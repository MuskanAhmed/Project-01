:: Tour Plan ::

Tour Duration : 4 Nights 3 Days (2 Nights stay at Standard / Deluxe Hotel Bandarban)

Day 01

Leave Dhaka by reserved car/coach for Bandarban.

Day 02 :

Hotel check in and refresh. After lunch, short sightseeing of Bandarban. Evening.. Back to hotel. Overnight at Bandarban.

Day 03 :

Start our journey to Nilgiri, This day we also visit Chimbuk and Shoilopropat. Back to the hotel. Overnight at Bandarban.

Day 04 :

This day we visit Sangu River, Swarna Mandir, Chakma Rajbari, Meghla Garden. At evening, leave Bandarban for Dhaka.

- End of Tour -
